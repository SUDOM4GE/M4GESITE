About this project

Hi! I’m a solo developer, and Minimalist is my very first big project—built entirely from scratch. Every line of code, every feature, every design choice comes straight from hours of learning, experimenting, and sometimes failing… and learning again. Minimalist isn’t just another messaging app. It’s a personal mission to create something simple, clean, and fully functional—without relying on pre-made templates or heavy frameworks. I learned everything along the way, from designing the interface to implementing the core functionality, and I continue to grow with every update. This project is a reflection of my passion for coding, problem-solving, and making tools that are intuitive, free, and minimal. Minimalist is more than an app—it’s the start of a journey, and I can’t wait to share it with you.
